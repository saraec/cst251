Lab 21: 2nd Draft Team Project Sketches

Subject of the Lab

The subject of this lab is to use the feedback I received from my group to modify the sketches that I created for the previous lab. The sketches will eventually be used for out Final Team Project.

Summary and Difficulties

This lab came together really quickly and easily. I got feedback from my teammates on my first round of sketches. They complimented my ideas: carousel, images in columns, and a background image for the header or under text. They suggested that side nav bars work better and that the designs with more images and less text worked better. There were a couple of sketches that I felt weren’t working or didn’t feel right, and they agreed. We also talked about other factors for the site as a whole. We agreed that there should be a recurring color and that one font should be used all the way through. I created three new sketches while incorporating things that worked with my first sketches and the suggestions that my team provided. I also tried to use more callouts that describe, more specifically, what will be on the site and what each element is.



Results

This lab was simple, but also neccessary. The feedback from my team was really useful in refining my designs. The second opinion helped me to realize what I liked and didn't like about the elements I had in my first round of sketches.
